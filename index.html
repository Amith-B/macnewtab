macNewTab Chrome Extension

A clean, macOS-inspired new tab experience for Chrome

About macNewTab

macNewTab is a lightweight Chrome extension that replaces your default new tab with a modern, macOS-style dashboard. It provides useful information at a glance and improves your productivity with a clean and elegant interface.

Core Features

  • ⏰ Elegant Widgets — macOS-style clock, date, and personalized greetings
  • 🔍 Spotlight Search — Quick web searches using your favorite engine
  • 🌤️ Weather Widget with auto/manual location
  • ⚡ Customizable Quick Links with drag-and-drop and custom icons
  • 🍅 Focus Studio with a Pomodoro timer and ambient sounds (White, Brown, Pink noise)
  • 📚 Bookmark Manager in a clean, Launchpad-like interface
  • 🗓️ Google Calendar — Expandable dashboard showing your upcoming events
  • 🗂️ Productivity Tools — Seamless Tab Manager and draggable Sticky Notes
  • 🎨 Customization — Minimal, responsive design with dynamic wallpapers and themes

Data Privacy and Usage

macNewTab uses Google Sign-In only to authenticate you securely. It requests access to your Google Calendar to display events. It also uses Chrome Sync to optionally save your Sticky Notes across your devices.

The extension does not transmit or share any user data to our servers. All calendar information and text notes stay within your browser or your personal Google account.

Data Usage Summary

  • Google Sign-In & Calendar API – Used only to securely fetch and display your upcoming events.
  • Chrome Sync – Used to securely sync your Sticky Notes text across your logged-in devices (optional).
  • Zero External Tracking – No personal data is sent to developer servers, logged, or shared with third parties.

macNewTab is available on the Chrome Web Store .
