About macNewTab

macNewTab is a lightweight Chrome extension that replaces your default new tab with a modern, macOS-style dashboard. It provides useful information at a glance and improves your productivity with a clean and elegant interface.

Core Features

  • macOS-style clock and date widget
  • Spotlight-style search bar for quick Google searches
  • Expandable dashboard showing your upcoming Google Calendar events
  • Minimal, responsive design with subtle macOS-inspired animations

Why macNewTab Uses Google OAuth

macNewTab uses Google Sign-In only to authenticate you securely. After authentication, the extension requests access to your Google Calendar so it can display your upcoming events directly on the new tab page.

The extension does not store, transmit, or share any user data. All calendar information is read-only and stays within your browser. No data is uploaded to any external servers.
