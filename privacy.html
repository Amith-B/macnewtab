Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

Overview

macNewTab is a Chrome extension that provides a macOS-style new tab experience, including widgets for clock, calendar, search, and quick links. This extension is designed for personal convenience and does not collect or store any personal information from users.

Google Sign-In

macNewTab uses Google Sign-In solely to allow users to access personalized Google service (such as calendar events) directly within the extension. Authentication happens securely through Google's own sign-in interface — the extension does not have access to your password or sensitive credentials.

Data Collection and Usage

macNewTab does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All information accessed via Google APIs (if any) is handled within your local browser session and is never transmitted to external servers by this extension.

Third-Party Services

The extension relies on official Google APIs and services for authentication and data access. Please review Google’s Privacy Policy for more details on how Google handles your account information.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the Chrome Web Store listing or GitHub repository.
